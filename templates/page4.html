Brief

A postpartum diet should focus on nutrient-rich foods to support healing and recovery after childbirth. Include a balance of lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats to replenish lost nutrients. Staying hydrated and eating small, frequent meals can help maintain energy levels and support breastfeeding.

How to follow

Plan meals with a mix of prioritize iron, calcium, and protein-rich options. Avoid skipping meals and keep healthy snacks ready for quick energy boosts.
